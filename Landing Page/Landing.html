About Banaras

Banaras, also known as Kashi, is one of the oldest continuously inhabited cities in the world. Located on the banks of the holy Ganga River in Uttar Pradesh, India, it is renowned for its spiritual, cultural, and historical significance. It's a city where every sunrise tells a story of spiritual awakening.

Spiritual Awakening

Experience the soulful mornings at Assi Ghat, listen to the chants, and feel the vibrations of divinity. Banaras offers a spiritual retreat like no other place in the world.

Banaras is considered the spiritual capital of India and is believed to be founded by Lord Shiva himself.
